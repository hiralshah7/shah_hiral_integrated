Who we are

At Home-Designing, we document inspiration. Our aim is to bring to our readers a steady set of resources that would help them visualize, create and maintain beautiful homes. Our emphasis is on visuals that motivate you. We cover architectural innovations, cool homes, ideas for specific rooms, new design trends, products and occasionally decor tips.
